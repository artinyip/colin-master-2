Our Manifesto —

Accessible.

Whether you're an emerging designer or an agency who needs something made in a pinch — manufacturing should be easy and accessible, not intimidating. Colin is here to guide & introduce you to the right people to get you started.

Efficient.

We get it, sometimes you spend more time searching for the right manufacturer than you spend designing. Our visual database of verified vendors will save you time when looking for a vendor that fits your needs.

Transparent.

We encourage designers to share projects they've created with their favorite vendors as a way to build a comprehensive database of great manufacturers every designer can rely on, whenever they need it.

Curated.

When we used to look for manufacturers in the Yellow Pages, it was pretty much a shot in the dark. With our vetted database, you know you're in good hands when you connect with a vendor.
